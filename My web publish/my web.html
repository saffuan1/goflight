AIRPORT DEPARTURES AND ARRIVALS


FLIGHTSTATS collects information from a large number of source (goverments, airlines, airport, reservation system, and other) and presents an intutive display of the data we compile. Registered users can explore datails collected from the data source. For more information, see an in-depth explanation below. In summary FlightStats:

  • Manage flight scedule
  • Tracks flight status in near real time
  • Reports both runway and gate times
  • Has global coverage
  • Provides exellent codeshare mapping
  • Links airport delay information to the affected flight
  • Stores information historically and calcullates on time performace rating for flights amd airlines
